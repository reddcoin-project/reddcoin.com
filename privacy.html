Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy, together with the Terms of Use posted on our Website, set forth the general rules and policies governing use of https://redd.love. Redd has always maintained a user-first and privacy-centric approach, and commits to all possible and reasonable efforts to respect user privacy and data, and we are vigilant and committed to this ethos. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, handle and process data of its customers and visitors’ personally identifiable information, should any be collected.

As a foundational member of the original blockchain and crypto community, Redd and the members of the teams that make up Redd commit to the following (with credit for inspiration from EM @ BlockFolio).

If you have any questions or concerns with Redd’s privacy policy, please feel free to email TechAdept, Project Leader for Redd. This Privacy Policy applies to our information collection, use, and disclosure practices for Website visitors ages 13 and over.
We have never and will never sell, rent, or trade on your user data.
This is completely against our ethos at Redd and a prime reason for the establishment of ReddCoin as a decentralized cryptocurrency payment system in 2014.
We do not collect any personal information from you.
User privacy is critically important to Redd. We will always take the necessary steps to protect your privacy in all the work Redd does. The information Users offer is always up to them to control, and it is Redd’s intention that such data never be misused.
We will always make best efforts to adhere to U.S. and GDPR (and other relevant) regulations.
We believe in being good citizens, both of our ecosystem and of the respective nations where we operate.We will always build products, websites, solutions, and partnerships with these ideals and ethics in mind first and foremost, and strive to provide a safe, secure and protected data environment for all members of our ReddHead community.
We will remove all of your data immediately upon request.
We are here to service our users first. If for any reason you would like your data removed, we’ll do so immediately without question. As mentioned, we typically do NOT collect any PII or sensitive data.
We will always work to enhance your control over your data.
Your data is yours, not ours. We have always and will always treat it this way.

Visiting Redd websites

As used in this policy, "Personally identifiable Information," "personal information," or "your information" is data that can identify a person or device. Aggregate data is not considered personal information. To improve user experience on our site, we may collect aggregate usage data from third party services (see Third Party Products and Services below). Redd is unlike most websites; our site does not collect your internet protocol address (IP address).

Third Party Products & Services

Redd does utilize third parties to obtain information from visitors of www.redd.love. For instance, if you interact with a third-party service while visiting Website, the applicable third-party service will send us certain Personal Data but only to the extent and defined as if the third-party service and your account settings allow such sharing. Personal Data or "personally identifiable information is defined as first and last names, physical addresses, email addresses, telephone numbers, social security numbers, other contact information shared with a business either physically or online, birthdates, details of physical appearance (height, weight, hair color), and any other information stored online that may identify an individual." The Personal Data Redd receives will depend on the policies and your account settings with the applicable third-party service. Redd uses this Personal Data based on legitimate business practices and in the interest of identifying trends and demographics to personalize and create an optimal user experience.

Redd is not responsible for the content or practices of websites, applications or services linked to Website. Also, interaction with any third-party website, application or service are subject to that third party’s own privacy procedures and terms of use.

Cookies

An HTTP cookie (also called web cookie, Internet cookie, browser cookie, or simply cookie) is a small piece of data stored on the user's computer by the web browser while browsing a website.

Cookies are sent to your browser via website and stored on your computer. Cookies may be enabled or disabled from the browser settings/preferences. Redd uses cookies via Google Analytics in order to measure and analyze website usage.

See Google’s Cookies Guide: https://developers.google.com/analytics/devguides/collection/analyticsjs/cookie-usage
